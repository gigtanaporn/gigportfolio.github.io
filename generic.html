Annual Reports

I initiated the creation of the annual report for the Facilities Services Department, a testament to my commitment to enhancing the visibility of our contributions across campus. This report serves as a platform for recognizing dedicated employees and celebrating year-long achievements. Leveraging Adobe InDesign and strategic mind mapping, I designed the report with engaging content and visual elements. Through collaborative efforts with multiple Facilities teams, I spotlighted major accomplishment stories that speak to our collective success.
