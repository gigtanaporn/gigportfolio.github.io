Tree Tour in BloNo

In this project, I played different roles - copywriter, web developer, and photographer. Leveraging the ArcGIS Story Map, I engaged audiences through a virtual, unveiling the historical legacy of Ginkgo trees nested within the Bloomington-Normal community. I also created printed media (tree tour sign and event flyer) and coordinated events to connect with target audiences. This initiative was a successful partnership between the Tree Campus/City Committees of Illinois State University, Westminster Village, the Town of Normal, and the City of Bloomington.

Marketing Strategy

Storytelling

Utilizing ArcGIS Story Map to tell the remarkable story of the Ginkgo trees within Bloomington-Normal and engage audiences with interactive content.
